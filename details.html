First Graduate Scholarship


Description

The First Graduate Scholarship is a government initiative that provides financial assistance to students who are the first graduates in their family. It aims to encourage higher education and reduce the financial burden on deserving students.

Eligibility

  • Must be the first graduate in the family.
  • Must be studying in a recognized college or university.
  • Must meet the government eligibility criteria.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for higher education.
  • Support for tuition fees.
  • Encourages students to continue their studies.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Community Certificate (if applicable)
  • College ID Card
  • Recent Passport Size Photo

Application Deadline

15 August 2026

How to Apply

  1. Check your eligibility.
  2. Collect all required documents.
  3. Visit the official scholarship portal.
  4. Fill in the application form.
  5. Upload the required documents.
  6. Submit the application before the deadline.
